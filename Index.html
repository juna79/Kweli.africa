The product

Kweli is the trust layer under EAX Global.

EAX shows what verified environmental markets can become. Kweli is the verification infrastructure that makes the underlying records provably real.

01

For issuers

Labs, programmes and institutions register cryptographic proof of a document without exposing the document itself. Your data stays yours.

02

For verifiers

Regulators, buyers and auditors scan a QR code and instantly know whether a certificate is valid — no account required, no technical knowledge needed.

03

For markets

Verified records become the foundation for trusted environmental assets, cleaner ESG reporting and higher institutional confidence across entire value chains.

How it works

Simple enough for a QR code.
Strong enough for an audit.

01

Create

A certificate or report is created inside the issuer's existing system. No integration required to start.

02

Hash

The file is fingerprinted client-side using SHA-256. The private document never leaves the issuer.

03

Anchor

The hash is recorded on blockchain with an RFC3161 timestamp for institutional-grade proof.

04

Link

A QR code or verification link is added to the document — scannable by anyone, anywhere.

05

Verify

Anyone scans and sees verified or not verified in under three seconds.

The technology

JSON runs it.
Blockchain proves it.

Kweli adds a proof layer beneath your existing systems. Institutions keep their databases, workflows and portals. Kweli simply makes the outputs verifiable.

Verification is free and public. Registration is the billable act.

Built for institutional workflows
#
SHA-256 document fingerprint Any change to the document creates a different hash, making tampering immediately visible to any verifier worldwide.
⛓
Celo blockchain anchoring Low-cost, high-throughput proof infrastructure suitable for high-volume certificate registration at institutional scale.
T
RFC 3161 timestamping A recognised, standards-compliant timestamp layer for compliance, audit trail and institutional review processes.
⌨
Built for integration Designed to plug into existing labs, registries, dashboards and enterprise systems with minimal friction.
