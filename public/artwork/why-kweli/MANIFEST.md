# Why Kweli page — Art Book asset manifest

Source: the Kweli Art Book contact sheets. Only the contact sheets exist
today — the frame below is not yet exported as a file.

Deliberately one placement, not several: the page's strongest moments (the
manifesto, "Kweli makes one promise") work as typography and graphic
weight, not imagery — adding a photograph there would compete with the
statement rather than support it. The single band below earns its place
because it grounds the abstract document-chain diagram that follows it in
something concrete before it gets iconographic.

## Band — after the intro statement, before the Ecosystem chain

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Placement | `CinematicBand` between `Intro` and `EcosystemChain` in `src/components/home/WhyKweli.tsx` |
| Source | Volume 1, frame 13 or 17 — many hands / cross-professional trust scene |
| Caption | "A document rarely stays where it was issued." |
| Export as | `why-kweli-many-hands.webp` |
| Crop | 21:9 desktop, 16:10 mobile, full-bleed, no embedded text |
| Why here | The DocumentChain diagram that follows is abstract (icons and labels). One real, human scene right before it gives the abstraction something to stand for, rather than opening cold into iconography. |

## Considered and rejected

- **Behind the Fingerprint manifesto** — the section is designed to work as
  a typographic pause (oversized statement, glow, icon). A photograph
  behind it would soften rather than sharpen that moment.
- **Behind "Kweli Makes One Promise"** — same reasoning; this statement
  needs to be read, not looked past.
